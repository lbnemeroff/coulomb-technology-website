How It Works

Solar In. Battery Stored. Home Powered.

The Series-R integrates seamlessly with your existing or new solar system — storing excess solar energy during the day and dispatching it at night or during outages.

1

Solar Panels Generate Power

Your rooftop solar panels generate DC electricity throughout the day — more than your home typically uses during peak sun hours.

2

Series-R Stores the Excess

Excess solar energy that would otherwise be exported to the grid (often at low rates) is stored in the Series-R battery instead.

3

EnergiOS™ Optimizes Dispatch

The app's AI engine decides when to use battery power vs. grid power — maximizing savings based on your utility's time-of-use rates.

4

Automatic Backup During Outages

When the grid goes down, the Series-R switches to backup mode in milliseconds — keeping your home powered without interruption.
