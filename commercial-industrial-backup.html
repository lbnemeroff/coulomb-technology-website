How We Solve Your Energy Challenges

01

Peak Demand Management

For: Manufacturing, Cold Storage, Distribution Centers, Agriculture

The Challenge

High demand charges can represent 30–70% of commercial electricity costs. Energy storage helps reduce these charges, but lithium-ion systems lose capacity in extreme temperatures — forcing oversizing and reducing ROI.

Our Solution

  • 279kWh BESS optimized for demand charge reduction and TOU arbitrage
  • Maintains rated capacity from −40°C to +55°C (no temperature derating)
  • Simpler thermal management = lower OPEX and fewer service calls
  • Scalable configurations for facilities from 500kW to multi-MW peak demand

03

Material Handling & Electric Mobility

For: Forklift Fleets, Airport Ground Support, Golf Courses, Industrial Vehicles

The Challenge

Material handling operations require batteries that deliver high power rapidly and handle constant charge/discharge cycles throughout shifts. Lead-acid batteries are heavy and maintenance-intensive. Lithium alternatives struggle in temperature extremes.

Our Solution

  • 48V traction battery engineered for high power delivery and fast discharge
  • Rapid power response for acceleration and performance demands
  • Multi-thousand cycle life handles demanding duty cycles
  • Operates reliably in cold storage (−20°C) and outdoor heat (+50°C)
  • Enhanced safety for enclosed operator compartments
