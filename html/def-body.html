Emergency Response

Emergency Response & Disaster Recovery

For: FEMA Operations, Disaster Relief Staging, Emergency Medical Services, Temporary Command Centers

The Challenge

Disaster response requires power infrastructure that can be deployed rapidly to compromised environments — often with no grid, extreme weather conditions, and zero tolerance for equipment failure. Diesel generators create fuel logistics burdens and noise signatures that complicate operations.

The Coulomb Technology Solution
  • Rapid deployment with no fuel logistics — store energy before the event, deploy instantly
  • Silent operation maintains noise discipline and reduces detection risk
  • Pairs with mobile solar for extended off-grid autonomy in the field
  • Operates reliably in flood, heat, and cold conditions without performance loss
  • Scalable from single-shelter power to multi-agency staging area support
