Complete Battery System

Everything You Need. Nothing Extra to Source.

The 48V Series R ships as a complete, ready-to-install wall-mount system. Compact enclosure, integrated BMS, LCD display, and all connections included — no additional components required.

  • Sodium-Ion NFPP Prismatic Cells (15S2P)

    Safer chemistry with less explosive properties — no fires seen to date during thermal runaway tests. Safe for indoor residential installation in garages, basements, and utility rooms.

  • Built-In BMS — 8 Intelligent Protections

    Real-time monitoring of overcharge, overcurrent, overdischarge, overheat, temperature, short circuit, balanced power, and reverse connection — continuously protecting cells and power supply.

  • LCD Display with System Monitoring

    On-unit LCD shows pack voltage, current, SOC, and energy in real time. On/Off switch and alarm indicators included — no separate monitoring hardware required.

  • Communication Interfaces

    CAN Bus, RS485, and Modbus RTU interfaces included for integration with inverters, solar charge controllers, and home energy management systems.

  • IP21 Rated Wall-Mount Enclosure

    Compact wall-mountable enclosure (790 × 520 × 175 mm) rated IP21 for indoor use. Designed for residential garages, basements, utility rooms, and light commercial installations.

Why Sodium-Ion for Home & Light Commercial

Where Lead-Acid and
Lithium Fall Short,
Sodium-Ion Delivers.

Lead-acid requires maintenance and degrades quickly. Lithium-ion carries thermal runaway risk and struggles in cold environments. The 48V Series R eliminates all three problems with sodium-ion NFPP chemistry — the safest, longest-lasting residential battery available.

01
Safety-First Design
Safer Chemistry Design

Safer chemistry with less explosive properties. Safe for indoor residential installation — no special ventilation required.

  • Zero thermal runaway incidents seen to date
  • No fires seen to date and no cell to cell propagation seen to date
  • No toxic gas emissions
  • Safe in garages, basements, and utility rooms
  • Next generation chemistries in progress with no thermal runaway design
02
Extreme Temperature Performance
Full Capacity in Any Climate

Reliable operation from −20°C to +60°C. Works in garages, basements, and outdoor enclosures where lithium-ion loses 20–40% of capacity in cold conditions.

  • Full capacity at −20°C — no cold-weather derating
  • No battery heating systems required
  • No thermal management complexity
  • Minimal derating across full temperature range
  • Ideal for cold-climate installations
03
Exceptional Longevity
8,000+ Cycles, 20-Year Life

8,000+ cycles at 80% depth of discharge and a 20-year design life provide decades of reliable backup power and solar storage — minimal degradation over time.

  • 8,000+ cycles @ 80% DoD
  • 20-year design life at 25°C
  • Minimal capacity degradation over time
  • No watering, no acid spills, no maintenance
  • Lower lifetime cost than lead-acid or lithium
