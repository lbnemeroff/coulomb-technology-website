Complete Traction Battery System

Everything You Need. Nothing Extra to Source.

The 48V Series M ships as a complete, ready-to-install traction battery pack. Rugged IP54-rated enclosure, integrated BMS, standard 48V terminals, and 150A continuous discharge — a direct drop-in replacement for lead-acid traction batteries.

  • Sodium-Ion NFPP Prismatic Cells (16S2P)

    Safer chemistry with less explosive properties — no fires seen to date during thermal runaway tests. Safe for enclosed vehicles including forklifts, golf carts, and airport tugs operating in warehouses, garages, and passenger areas.

  • Built-In BMS — 8 Intelligent Protections

    Real-time monitoring of overcharge, overcurrent, overdischarge, overheat, temperature, short circuit, balanced power, and reverse connection — continuously protecting cells and the vehicle's power supply.

  • IP54-Rated Rugged Enclosure

    Dust-protected and splash-resistant IP54 enclosure (790 × 520 × 210 mm) built to withstand the vibration, shock, and moisture exposure of daily traction use in industrial and outdoor environments.

  • Standard 48V Terminals — Direct Drop-In

    Standard 48V battery terminals allow direct replacement of existing lead-acid traction batteries with no wiring modifications. Works with all standard 48V chargers and vehicle controllers.

  • 150A Continuous Discharge — High-Power Traction

    50% higher continuous discharge current than the Series-R, delivering the sustained high-current output required by forklifts, airport ground support equipment, and heavy-duty utility vehicles.

Why Sodium-Ion for Electric Mobility

Where Lead-Acid and
Lithium Fall Short,
Sodium-Ion Delivers.

Lead-acid traction batteries require regular watering, equalization charges, and degrade rapidly under deep cycling. Lithium-ion carries thermal runaway risk — a serious liability in enclosed vehicles and passenger transport. The 48V Series M eliminates all three problems with sodium-ion NFPP chemistry: maintenance-free, and built for 8,000+ deep cycles.

Safer Chemistry Design

Safer chemistry with less explosive properties — safe for enclosed vehicles, passenger transport, and indoor warehouse operation.

  • Zero thermal runaway incidents seen to date
  • No fires seen to date and no cell to cell propagation seen to date
  • No toxic gas emissions in enclosed spaces
  • Safe for passenger-carrying vehicles
  • Next generation chemistries in progress with no thermal runaway design

Operates reliably from −20°C to +45°C with no thermal management system required. Performs in cold storage warehouses, outdoor environments, and high-temperature loading docks without derating or performance loss.

  • Vibration and shock resistant (IP54)
