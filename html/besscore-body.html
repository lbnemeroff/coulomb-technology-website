The Chemistry

Sodium-Ion NFPP: Why It's Different.

Not all sodium-ion batteries are the same. Coulomb Technology uses NFPP (Na₃Fe₂(PO₄)₃) cathode chemistry — a specific formulation that delivers superior cycle life, thermal stability, and cost economics compared to other sodium-ion chemistries and all lithium-ion alternatives.

Non-Flammable by Chemistry

NFPP cathode chemistry eliminates the oxygen release mechanism that causes lithium-ion thermal runaway. No oxygen = no fire. This is a fundamental chemistry advantage, not a safety add-on.

Superior Cycle Life

NFPP's stable crystal structure maintains capacity through 4,000–6,000 full cycles — significantly outperforming LFP (2,000–3,000 cycles) and NMC (1,000–2,000 cycles) in high-frequency cycling applications.

Extreme Temperature Performance

NFPP maintains >95% capacity from −40°C to +55°C — eliminating the need for HVAC systems and enabling deployment in environments where lithium-ion simply cannot operate reliably.

Domestic Supply Chain

Sodium is the fourth most abundant element on Earth, with the largest reserves in the United States. No cobalt, no lithium, no geopolitical exposure — and no 45% tariff risk on raw materials.

Maintenance-Free Operation

No liquid cooling, no active thermal management, no electrolyte maintenance. NFPP cells are sealed, solid-state, and designed for the full 15–20 year system life without scheduled maintenance interventions.

Scalable Architecture

NFPP cell modules stack from 12V (Series-S) to 48V (Series-M) to 279V (Series-DC) — enabling the same chemistry to serve residential backup, commercial peak shaving, and utility-scale grid services from a single platform.

Integration Architecture

Open Protocol. Your Controls. Our Battery.

Core BESS systems are designed to integrate with any third-party EMS, SCADA, or building management system. Our BMS exposes a full suite of communication interfaces — so your integration team can be up and running in days, not months.

Modbus TCP/IP
Full register map for battery state, cell voltages, temperatures, alarms, and control commands. Industry-standard protocol supported by virtually every EMS and SCADA platform.
CAN Bus
High-speed CAN 2.0B interface for real-time BMS data and control — ideal for motive, vehicle, and industrial automation applications requiring low-latency communication.
RS485 / Modbus RTU
Serial Modbus RTU for legacy SCADA systems, building automation controllers, and industrial PLCs. Supports multi-drop configurations for multi-unit installations.
BACnet IP
Native BACnet/IP support for integration with commercial building management systems (BMS) — enabling seamless coordination with HVAC, lighting, and other building systems.
REST API
JSON REST API for cloud-connected applications, custom dashboards, and software integrations. Full documentation and SDKs available for rapid development.
OCPP 2.0
Open Charge Point Protocol support for EV charging integration — enabling coordinated EV charging and battery dispatch for fleet and campus applications.

Integration documentation, register maps, and SDK access are available to qualified system integrators. Contact our engineering team to request access.
