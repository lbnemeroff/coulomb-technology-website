Complete Battery System

Everything Your Fleet Needs. Nothing Extra to Source.

The 48V Traction Battery ships as a complete, ready-to-install system. Drop it into your existing battery compartment and charge with your existing infrastructure — no modifications required.

  • Sodium-Ion NFPP Cells

    Prismatic cell configuration optimized for high discharge rates and deep cycle durability — no thermal runaway, no fire risk in enclosed spaces.

  • Integrated BMS — 8 Intelligent Protections

    Real-time cell monitoring with over-voltage, under-voltage, over-current, short circuit, over-temperature, under-temperature, overcharge, and deep discharge protection.

  • Opportunity & Fast Charge Capability

    Charge during breaks without damaging the battery — no memory effect, no degradation from partial charging. Eliminate battery swapping and reduce fleet footprint.

  • Rugged Vibration-Resistant Construction

    Designed for the shock and vibration of material handling environments — forklifts, AGVs, pallet jacks, and outdoor equipment.

  • Standard Drop-In Mounting

    Compatible with existing battery compartments for Class I, II, and III electric forklifts — CAN Bus and Modbus communication protocols supported.

Ideal Applications

Built for Every Material Handling & Fleet Operation

From freezer warehouses to airport tarmacs, the 48V Traction Battery maintains full performance where lead-acid and lithium fall short.

Forklift Fleets

Class I, II & III Electric Forklifts

Drop-in replacement for reach trucks, counterbalance forklifts, order pickers, and pallet jacks. Opportunity charging eliminates battery swapping and swap rooms.

  • No battery swapping — one battery per truck
  • Full capacity in cold storage and freezer environments
  • No fire suppression required in enclosed warehouses
Automation

AGVs & Automated Material Handling

Designed for the continuous operation cycles of AGVs, AMRs, tuggers, and tow tractors. Fast charging and 6,000+ cycle life support 24/7 automated operations.

  • Fast charge during automated routing pauses
  • 6,000+ cycles for multi-year continuous operation
  • CAN Bus and Modbus integration with WMS/WCS systems
Cold Storage

Freezer & Cold Storage Operations

Maintains full rated capacity from −20°C to −30°C where lithium-ion loses 20–40% of capacity. No battery heating systems required — lower auxiliary power draw and simpler operations.

  • Full capacity at −20°C — no cold-weather derating
  • No battery heating systems required
  • Ideal for food distribution, cold chain, and pharma
Outdoor Fleet

Airport GSE & Outdoor Equipment

Wide operating temperature range for year-round outdoor operation — aircraft tugs, baggage tractors, ground power units, and port terminal equipment.

  • Operates from −20°C to +60°C without derating
  • Non-flammable — safe near aircraft and fuel
  • No thermal management complexity for outdoor fleets
