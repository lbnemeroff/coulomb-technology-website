Why Sodium-Ion for 12V Applications

The End of Lead-Acid.
No Compromise.

Lead-acid batteries require regular maintenance, off-gas hydrogen during charging, and degrade rapidly when left partially discharged. Lithium-ion alternatives carry thermal runaway risk — a serious concern in enclosed RV and marine spaces. The 12V Series S eliminates all these problems: maintenance-free sodium-ion chemistry, non-flammable, and rated for 4,000+ deep cycles at 80% DOD.

Non-Flammable Chemistry

Sodium-ion NFPP cells cannot thermally run away. No fire risk in your RV, boat, or vehicle — even in the event of a short circuit, overcharge, or physical damage. Safe for enclosed spaces where lithium is prohibited.

All-Temperature Operation

Operates from −20°C to +60°C — outperforming lead-acid in cold starts and lithium-ion in high-heat environments. Reliable performance whether you're in the Arctic or the desert.

Zero Maintenance

No watering, no equalization charges, no acid spills, no terminal corrosion. Install it and forget it. The built-in BMS handles cell balancing automatically — your battery is always ready when you need it.

Applications

Built for Every 12V Application.

From weekend RV trips to year-round marine liveaboards, the Series-S delivers reliable 12V power wherever lead-acid batteries have been used — without the maintenance, weight, or fire risk.

RV & Motorhome

House bank replacement for Class A, B, and C motorhomes. No off-gassing in enclosed cabins, no watering, and 4× the cycle life of lead-acid — perfect for full-time RV living.

Marine & Boat

Starting and deep-cycle house batteries for sailboats, powerboats, and fishing vessels. IP55-rated enclosure handles marine humidity and vibration. Non-toxic — safe in bilge compartments.

Off-Grid Solar

12V solar storage for cabins, tiny homes, and remote installations. Compatible with all standard MPPT and PWM solar charge controllers. Operates in extreme temperatures where lead-acid fails.

Mobility & Light EV

Power source for mobility scooters, electric wheelchairs, golf caddies, and light electric vehicles. Lighter than lead-acid, longer cycle life, and zero maintenance for daily-use mobility equipment.
