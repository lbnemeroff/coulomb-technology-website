Complete 12V Battery System

Everything Included. Nothing to Maintain.

The 12V Series S ships as a complete, ready-to-install battery. Built-in BMS, standard lead-acid terminal posts, and sodium-ion chemistry that never needs watering, equalization, or acid checks — just install and forget.

  • Sodium-Ion NFPP Prismatic Cells

    Non-flammable, non-toxic sodium-ion chemistry — zero thermal runaway risk, no acid, no off-gassing. Safe for enclosed spaces including RV cabins, boat bilges, and enclosed vehicle compartments.

  • Built-In BMS — 8 Intelligent Protections

    Real-time monitoring of overcharge, overcurrent, overdischarge, overheat, temperature, short circuit, cell balancing, and reverse connection — continuously protecting your battery and connected equipment.

  • Compact ABS Enclosure — Standard BCI Footprint

    Fits standard 12V battery trays and hold-downs. The 100Ah unit weighs just 8.5 kg — up to 60% lighter than an equivalent lead-acid battery — making installation and handling effortless.

  • Standard Lead-Acid Terminal Posts — True Drop-In

    Standard M6 terminal posts allow direct replacement of any 12V lead-acid battery with no wiring modifications. Compatible with all standard 12V chargers, inverters, and solar charge controllers.

  • Three Capacity Options — 30Ah, 60Ah, 100Ah

    Choose the right capacity for your application. Stack multiple units in parallel for larger systems — all three models share the same BMS communication protocol and charging profile for seamless parallel operation.

Why Sodium-Ion for 12V Applications

The End of Lead-Acid.
No Compromise.

Lead-acid batteries require regular maintenance, off-gas hydrogen during charging, and degrade rapidly when left partially discharged. Lithium-ion alternatives carry thermal runaway risk — a serious concern in enclosed RV and marine spaces. The 12V Series S eliminates all these problems: maintenance-free sodium-ion chemistry, non-flammable, and rated for 4,000+ deep cycles at 80% DOD.

01
Non-Flammable Chemistry
Zero Thermal Runaway Risk

Sodium-ion NFPP cells cannot thermally run away. No fire risk in your RV, boat, or vehicle — even in the event of a short circuit, overcharge, or physical damage. Safe for enclosed spaces where lithium is prohibited.

  • Zero thermal runaway incidents to date
  • Non-hazardous shipping — air-shippable
  • No toxic gas emissions in enclosed spaces
  • Safe in bilge compartments and RV cabins
02
All-Temperature Operation
−20°C to +60°C Range

Operates from −20°C to +60°C — outperforming lead-acid in cold starts and lithium-ion in high-heat environments. Reliable performance whether you're in the Arctic or the desert.

  • −20°C to +60°C operating range
  • No active thermal management required
  • Reliable cold-weather cranking
  • Stable in high-heat vehicle compartments
03
Zero Maintenance
Install and Forget

No watering, no equalization charges, no acid spills, no terminal corrosion. The built-in BMS handles cell balancing automatically — your battery is always ready when you need it.

  • No watering or equalization required
  • No acid leaks or terminal corrosion
  • Works with standard 12V chargers
  • 10-year design life at 25°C
