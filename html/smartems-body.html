The Problem With Conventional BESS

A Battery Alone Is Just a Battery.

Most commercial energy storage systems ship with a BMS and a basic monitoring app — and that's it. The result is a passive asset that only does what you tell it to do, when you remember to tell it.

Reactive, Not Predictive

Standard systems respond to what's happening now — they can't anticipate tomorrow's peak demand window or tonight's low-price charging opportunity.

Manual Intervention Required

Operators must manually adjust charge schedules, respond to grid events, and enroll in demand response programs — creating operational burden and missed revenue.

Revenue Left on the Table

Without automated grid services enrollment, most commercial BESS owners never participate in demand response, capacity markets, or VPP programs — missing thousands per month.

Grid Services & Revenue Platform

GridIQ™

Your battery as a revenue asset. GridIQ™ connects your system to demand response programs, capacity markets, frequency regulation services, and virtual power plant networks — automatically enrolling and dispatching to earn revenue around the clock.

Demand Response Enrollment Automatically enrolls your system in utility demand response programs and dispatches stored energy during grid stress events — earning payments with no action on your part.
Capacity Market Participation Bid your battery's capacity into ISO/RTO capacity markets to earn recurring monthly revenue — a predictable income stream that shortens payback periods.
Frequency Regulation Provide fast-response frequency regulation services to grid operators — one of the highest-value grid services, enabled by sodium-ion's rapid response characteristics.
Virtual Power Plant (VPP) Networks Join a VPP aggregation network to earn additional revenue during grid stress events — coordinated with thousands of other distributed assets for maximum grid value.
How It Works

Your Battery Works While You Sleep.

Coulomb AI™ continuously monitors solar generation, grid pricing, weather forecasts, and your facility's load profile — then automatically executes the best energy strategy every 15 minutes, 24/7.

01

Monitor

EnergiOS™ continuously reads your facility's energy consumption, solar generation, battery state of charge, and live grid pricing signals — building a real-time picture of your energy environment.

02

Predict

Coulomb AI™ uses machine learning to forecast the next 24–48 hours of energy demand, solar output, and utility pricing — identifying the optimal windows to charge from the grid and discharge to your facility.

03

Optimize

The system automatically executes the optimal strategy — charging during low-cost periods, discharging during peak demand windows, and reserving capacity for backup power or grid services as needed.

04

Earn

GridIQ™ simultaneously enrolls your system in demand response, capacity markets, and VPP programs — stacking revenue streams on top of your utility bill savings for the highest possible financial return.

Why Sodium-Ion?

The Battery That Solves What Lithium Can't.

Three critical problems hold back lithium-ion in commercial and industrial applications. Coulomb Technology's sodium-ion NFPP chemistry eliminates all three.

Lithium-Ion
✗ Thermal runaway risk — fire & explosion hazard in enclosed spaces
✗ Performance degrades below 32°F — requires costly HVAC systems
✗ Cobalt & lithium supply chain — tariff exposure, price volatility
✗ 10–15 year projected lifespan under commercial duty cycles
✗ Imported materials — limited DOD/Federal compliance pathway
Coulomb Technology Sodium-Ion
✓ Non-flammable NFPP chemistry — zero thermal runaway, no fire suppression required
✓ Operates -40°F to 140°F — no HVAC required, lower TCO in all climates
✓ Sodium is 1,000× more abundant than lithium — stable pricing, no tariff risk
✓ 20-year projected battery life — verified duty-cycle testing
✓ Made in America — DOD/Federal compliance pathway available
