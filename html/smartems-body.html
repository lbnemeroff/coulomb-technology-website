What Is Smart EMS?

Energy Storage That Thinks for Itself.

A Smart EMS (Energy Management System) is a fully integrated energy storage platform — not just a battery cabinet. It combines a high-performance sodium-ion BESS with three proprietary software layers that continuously monitor your facility, predict grid pricing, and automatically execute the optimal charge/discharge strategy every 15 minutes, 24 hours a day.

The result: your energy costs go down, your revenue goes up, and your operations stay running regardless of what the grid does. No manual intervention. No utility surprises. No downtime.

✓ Real-time energy monitoring via web & mobile app
✓ AI-driven charge/discharge optimization — 24/7
✓ Automated demand charge reduction & energy arbitrage
✓ Grid services enrollment & revenue generation via GridIQ™
✓ Remote diagnostics, firmware updates, & proactive alerts
Intelligent Software Stack

Three Engines. One Seamless System.

Every Coulomb Technology Smart EMS ships with three proprietary software layers that work together to maximize the value of your energy storage investment — from day one and for the life of the system.

01
Energy Management System

EnergiOS™

The operating system for your energy assets. EnergiOS™ is the command center that gives you complete visibility and control over your energy storage system — from anywhere, at any time.

Web Dashboard & Mobile App Real-time battery state, energy flow visualization, and historical performance data — accessible from any device for operators, property managers, and tenants.
Automated Dispatch & Scheduling Set charge/discharge schedules, peak shaving targets, and backup reserve levels — or let Coulomb AI™ handle everything automatically.
Remote Diagnostics & OTA Updates Proactive health monitoring, fault alerts, and over-the-air firmware updates ensure your system is always running at peak performance without on-site visits.
Multi-Site Management Manage a portfolio of systems from a single dashboard — ideal for property managers, fleet operators, and commercial real estate portfolios with multiple facilities.
02
Predictive Optimization Engine

Coulomb AI™

The brain of your energy system. Coulomb AI™ is a machine learning engine that continuously learns your facility's load profile, predicts utility pricing, and automatically executes the optimal charge/discharge strategy — maximizing savings and revenue with zero manual intervention required.

Load Profile Learning Coulomb AI™ analyzes months of historical consumption data to build an accurate model of your facility's energy demand — and gets smarter every day.
Real-Time Price Forecasting Integrates with utility rate structures, day-ahead energy markets, and demand charge windows to predict the optimal times to charge from the grid and discharge to your facility.
Automated Demand Charge Reduction Automatically dispatches stored energy during peak demand windows to flatten your facility's demand curve — eliminating the largest line item on your commercial utility bill.
Solar + Storage Co-Optimization When paired with solar, Coulomb AI™ maximizes self-consumption, minimizes export curtailment, and coordinates solar and battery dispatch for the highest possible financial return.
03
Grid Services & Revenue Platform

GridIQ™

Your battery as a revenue asset. GridIQ™ connects your Coulomb Technology system to demand response programs, capacity markets, frequency regulation services, and virtual power plant networks — automatically enrolling and dispatching to earn revenue around the clock.

Demand Response Enrollment GridIQ™ automatically enrolls your system in utility demand response programs and dispatches stored energy during grid stress events — earning payments without any action on your part.
Capacity Market Participation Bid your battery's capacity into ISO/RTO capacity markets to earn recurring monthly revenue — a predictable income stream that improves project economics and shortens payback periods.
Frequency Regulation Provide fast-response frequency regulation services to grid operators — one of the highest-value grid services available, enabled by the rapid response characteristics of sodium-ion chemistry.
Virtual Power Plant (VPP) Networks Join a VPP aggregation network to earn additional revenue during grid stress events — your battery works for you around the clock, coordinated with thousands of other distributed assets.
How It Works

Your Battery Works While You Sleep.

Coulomb AI™ continuously monitors solar generation, grid pricing, weather forecasts, and your facility's load profile — then automatically executes the best energy strategy every 15 minutes, 24/7.

01

Monitor

EnergiOS™ continuously reads your facility's energy consumption, solar generation (if present), battery state of charge, and live grid pricing signals — building a real-time picture of your energy environment.

02

Predict

Coulomb AI™ uses machine learning to forecast the next 24–48 hours of energy demand, solar output, and utility pricing — identifying the optimal windows to charge from the grid and discharge to your facility.

03

Optimize

The system automatically executes the optimal strategy — charging during low-cost periods, discharging during peak demand windows, and reserving capacity for backup power or grid services as needed.

04

Earn

GridIQ™ simultaneously enrolls your system in demand response, capacity markets, and VPP programs — stacking revenue streams on top of your utility bill savings for the highest possible financial return.
