Sustainability

Renewable Integration & Self-Consumption

For: Manufacturing, Agriculture, Retail, Corporate Campuses

The Challenge

Solar generation peaks at midday; facility demand peaks in the afternoon. Without storage, excess solar is exported at low rates while facilities still pay peak prices for grid power during their highest-demand hours.

The Coulomb Technology Solution
  • Store midday solar surplus and deploy it during afternoon peak demand windows
  • Increase solar self-consumption from ~30% to 85%+ with optimized dispatch
  • Qualify for SGIP, ITC, and state-level storage incentives
  • CoulombAI™ optimizes solar + storage dispatch in real time against utility rates
