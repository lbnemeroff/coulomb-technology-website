Material Handling

Electric Forklifts & Material Handling

For: Distribution Centers, Cold Storage Warehouses, Food & Beverage Facilities, Manufacturing Plants

The Challenge

Forklift fleets operating in cold storage and freezer environments face a critical problem: lithium-ion batteries lose 20–40% of their capacity below 0°C, forcing operators to oversize battery packs, add heating systems, or accept reduced shift coverage. Thermal incidents in enclosed warehouses create unacceptable safety and insurance risks.

The Coulomb Technology Solution
  • Full rated capacity from -40°C to +55°C — no cold-weather performance loss
  • Inherently non-flammable chemistry eliminates warehouse fire risk
  • No battery heating systems required — lower auxiliary power draw
  • Supports multi-shift operations with opportunity charging between shifts
  • 8,000+ cycle life reduces total cost of ownership vs. lead-acid and lithium

Fleet Charging

Commercial EV Fleet Charging

For: Trucking Fleets, Transit Agencies, Utility Fleets, Corporate Vehicle Fleets, Rental Fleets

The Challenge

Large commercial EV fleets create massive demand spikes when multiple vehicles charge simultaneously — driving up demand charges, straining grid connections, and requiring expensive utility upgrades. Fleet operators need energy storage that can buffer peak demand, reduce charging costs, and maintain uptime through grid outages.

The Coulomb Technology Solution
  • Behind-the-meter BESS eliminates demand charge spikes from simultaneous charging
  • Enables fleet charging without costly utility service upgrades
  • Stores off-peak energy to reduce charging cost per vehicle mile
  • Backup power maintains charging operations during grid outages
  • Pairs with on-site solar for net-zero depot operations
