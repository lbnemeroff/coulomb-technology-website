Applications

Defense & Government Applications

01

Remote Military Installations

The Challenge

Remote and forward operating bases face extreme climate conditions, limited resupply logistics, and critical power requirements for communications, surveillance, and personnel safety. Battery systems must perform reliably from Arctic cold to desert heat without frequent service interventions.

Our Solution

  • 279kWh BESS designed for extreme environment operation
  • Proven −40°C to +55°C performance for global deployment zones
  • Reduced thermal management complexity lowers auxiliary power draw
  • Modular design supports rapid deployment and field serviceability
  • Enhanced safety for personnel and equipment protection
Typical Applications: Forward operating bases, Arctic deployments, desert environments, renewable hybrid microgrids, communications stations
02

Critical Government Infrastructure

The Challenge

Government facilities — from emergency operations centers to border security installations — require uninterrupted power for public safety and national security. Backup systems must meet stringent safety standards and perform reliably during extended outages.

Our Solution

  • Configurable runtime (2–10 hours) for mission-specific requirements
  • UL 9540A testing supports safety compliance for occupied facilities
  • Consistent performance independent of HVAC or climate conditions
  • Proactive monitoring and diagnostics for preventive maintenance
Typical Applications: Emergency operations centers, border security, 911 dispatch, government data centers, water treatment facilities
03

Mobile & Tactical Power Systems

The Challenge

Mobile operations and tactical deployments require portable, reliable power that can handle rough handling, rapid deployment cycles, and operation in any climate. Safety is paramount when batteries are used in close proximity to personnel.

Our Solution

  • Enhanced safety chemistry reduces thermal incident risk near personnel
  • Robust performance through transport and field deployment cycles
  • Wide operating temperature range for global mission flexibility
  • Air-shippable configurations meet transport safety requirements
Typical Applications: Portable power systems, mobile command platforms, tactical vehicle starter batteries
In development with U.S. Navy for tactical vehicle starter battery applications
