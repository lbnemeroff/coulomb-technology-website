Overview

Built for Fleet Performance in Any Environment

Lead-acid batteries hold your fleet back — heavy, maintenance-intensive, and unreliable in temperature extremes. Coulomb's sodium-ion traction batteries deliver the power, cycle life, and safety your operations demand, whether you're running a golf course fleet in summer heat or a cold-storage warehouse in sub-zero conditions.

All-Weather Operation
Full rated capacity from −40°C to +55°C — no performance loss in cold storage or outdoor heat
Zero Maintenance
No watering, no equalization, no sulfation — drop in and forget it
Longer Range Per Charge
Full capacity delivered in any temperature means more rounds, more shifts, more uptime
Enhanced Safety
No thermal runaway risk — safe in enclosed operator compartments and indoor charging areas
