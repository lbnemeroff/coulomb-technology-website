Material handling requires batteries that deliver high power rapidly through constant charge/discharge cycles. Lead-acid is heavy and maintenance-intensive. Lithium alternatives struggle in temperature extremes common in cold storage and outdoor environments.

  • 48V traction battery engineered for high power delivery and fast discharge
