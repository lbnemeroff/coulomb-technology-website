Why Sodium-Ion

The Battery Chemistry That Changes Everything

Sodium is 1,000× more abundant than lithium. Our NFPP (sodium iron phosphate pyrophosphate) chemistry delivers superior safety, extreme temperature performance, and a 20-year design life — at a lower total cost of ownership than any lithium-ion alternative.

No thermal runaway. No fire suppression required. No HVAC. No supply chain dependency on lithium, cobalt, or nickel. Just reliable, bankable energy storage.

Zero Thermal Runaway
NFPP chemistry is inherently non-flammable. No thermal runaway risk — ever. Safe near people, assets, and sensitive infrastructure.
−40°C to +55°C
Full performance across the complete military and industrial temperature range — no derating, no HVAC required.
20-Year Design Life
6,000+ cycle life at 80% DoD. Designed to outlast the solar assets it's paired with — reducing replacement costs.
14% Lower TCO
Lower acquisition cost, zero maintenance, no fuel logistics, and a 20-year life combine for the lowest TCO in the market.
